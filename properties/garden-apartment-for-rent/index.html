Garden Apartment

📍 Merl, Luxembourg

🏪 2 Bedrooms

A truly rare rental — a spacious ground-floor apartment with direct access to a private, fully enclosed garden. Light-filled interiors open seamlessly onto the outdoor space, perfect for those seeking a house feel with apartment convenience.

Features

  • Bright open-plan living and dining room
  • Large patio doors opening onto 80m² private garden
  • Two double bedrooms, both garden-facing
  • Modern bathroom with walk-in shower
  • Separate WC
  • Private garden shed and outdoor furniture included
  • Cellar storage and bicycle room

Location

Merl is one of Luxembourg City’s most desirable residential areas, bordered by the Merl Park and quiet tree-lined streets. Close to international schools, the Cloche d’Or shopping centre, and excellent public transport.
