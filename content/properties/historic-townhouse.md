One of the finest historic properties to come to market in Luxembourg City. This exceptional townhouse has been painstakingly restored by specialist craftsmen, preserving every original detail while integrating discreet modern comforts.

## Features

- Five floors including owner's apartment, guest suite, and studio  
- Original stone staircase and parquet floors throughout  
- Period fireplaces in principal rooms — fully functioning  
- Formal reception rooms with ornate cornicing  
- Contemporary kitchen fitted sympathetically within historic shell  
- Private walled courtyard garden  
- Wine cellar spanning the full footprint of the building  
- Double garage via private lane to the rear  

## Location

Ville Haute is Luxembourg City's historic heart — a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The property sits on one of the old town's most distinguished streets, steps from the Grand Ducal Palace, the Chamber of Deputies, and the finest restaurants and boutiques in the capital.
