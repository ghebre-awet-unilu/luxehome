Step back in time in this beautifully preserved stone cottage in Echternach. Original features — stone walls, oak beams, terracotta floors — give the property a warmth and authenticity that modern builds simply cannot replicate.

## Features

- Three bedrooms with original oak beams  
- Renovated bathroom with freestanding bath  
- Country kitchen with stone hearth  
- Front and rear flower gardens  
- Private courtyard with stone well feature  
- Wood-burning stove in the living room  
- Cellar for wine storage  
- Off-street parking for two cars  

## Location

Echternach is Luxembourg's oldest town and sits beside the picturesque Sure river on the German border. Known for its Benedictine Abbey, the European Music Festival, and gateway to the Little Switzerland hiking trails.
