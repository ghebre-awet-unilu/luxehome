## Who We Are

LuxeHome Real Estate is a Luxembourg-based property agency specialising in residential sales and rentals across the Grand Duchy. Founded in 2008, we have built a reputation for honest advice, deep local knowledge, and genuine care for our clients.

Whether you are buying your first home, investing in a rental property, or relocating to Luxembourg for work, our team guides you through every step of the process — from the first viewing to the final signature.

## Our Services

- **Residential Sales** — apartments, villas, penthouses, and family homes across Luxembourg City and the surrounding communes
- **Rentals** — long-term rental properties with full management support
- **Investment Advice** — market analysis and portfolio guidance for property investors
- **Relocation Assistance** — dedicated support for international clients and expat families

## Why Choose Us

- Over 15 years of experience in the Luxembourg property market
- Multilingual team — we speak English, French, German, and Luxembourgish
- Transparent fees with no hidden costs
- End-to-end support from search to completion
